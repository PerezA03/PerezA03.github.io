Recipe #1

  • Time:15 min
  • Serves:fro 1 kid
  1. 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  2. 2 tablespoons honey
  3. 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4. 2 tablespoons miniature semisweet chocolate chips
  5. 4 slices whole wheat bread
  6. 1 medium banana, thinly sliced
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1:Mix peanut butter, honey and cinnamon Step 2:stir in chocolate chips. Spread over bread. Layer two bread slices with banana slicesStep 3:top with remaining bread. If desired, cut into shapes using cookie cutters.

Recipe #2

  • Time:20 min
  • Serves:fro 1 kid
  1. 1 package (12 ounces) dark chocolate chips
  2. 24 pretzel rods
  3. 1 cup granola without raisins
Ingredients Quantity

Step 1:In a microwave, melt chocolate chips in a 2-cup glass measuring cup Step 2: stir until smooth. Pour into one side of a large shallow dish.Step 3:Roll each pretzel halfway into chocolate. Allow excess coating to drip off, then sprinkle pretzels with granola. Place on waxed paper until set. Store in an airtight container.
